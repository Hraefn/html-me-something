Goodbye, St. Louis!

We are packed and ready for an adventure! This is the first major vacation my husband and I have had without the kids. Needless to say, we are super stoked! We left St. Louis about 8:00 p.m. on November 1st. We drove through the night and stopped for a brief nap at a rest stop somewhere in Nebraska before powering on.

Whoa, Nebraska, Wyoming, Utah, and Nevada! I need an oil change...

Uh, well, we did a little driving, only stopping for some offroad adventures in Wyoming. Salt Lake was really pretty!

We made it to the tide pools!

I love my car, but after 30+ hours we were both ready to be pretty much anywhere else. There's only so much "I Spy" one can play, you know? We watched the sunset on the beach and marveled at the sight and smell of the ocean. It was really cool to see all the little critters in the tide pools at Duxbury Reef.
